51 This is the book of the generations of Adam In the day that God
created man in the likeness of God made he him 52 Male and female
created he them and blessed them and called their name Adam in the
day when they were created

53 And Adam lived an hundred and thirty years and begat a son in his
own likeness and after his image and called his name Seth 54 And
the days of Adam after he had begotten Seth were eight hundred years
and he begat sons and daughters 55 And all the days that Adam lived
were nine hundred and thirty years and he died

56 And Seth lived an hundred and five years and begat Enos 57 And
Seth lived after he begat Enos eight hundred and seven years and
begat sons and daughters 58 And all the days of Seth were nine
hundred and twelve years and he died
